Let others know where you visited while you were infected.

This part of COVID Contact is meant to be used by those who are infected with COVID-19. This is to help others know whether they have been in contact with you while you were infected.

Enter your name, and all the locations you have visited in the last two weeks as well as the dates you visited those locations to let people know where you have been while you had COVID-19. Click "Add Another Visited Location" to add another row of a location and a date that you have visited. Click "Add a Location to Location List" to add a new location to the list of available locations. Click "Submit your Visited Locations" to send your information to our database of visited locations and those infected with COVID-19.
