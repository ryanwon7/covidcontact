COVID Contact

An application to help you evaluate your potential exposure to the COVID-19 virus.

About Us

The COVID Contact application is designed to inform and evaluate your potential risk to the COVID-19 virus. The website does so through five main features, which are displayed and explained below.

COVID-19 Information

This feature provides general information about the COVID-19 virus, and gives best practices and tips on what to do to avoid transmission of the coronavirus, and minimize the chance that you receive the coronavirus. Helpful videos and links to other sites regarding official statements on COVID-19 and best practices are also provided.

Symptom Analysis

The Symptom Analysis portion of the website is useful for those who would like to determine the likelihood that they currently have the coronavirus based on their current symptoms. Users are asked to respond to various questions regarding their health and symptoms, and will be given an analysis that states the likelihood that their symptoms are from COVID-19. This is not a subsitute for an actual medical test, but will be a good indicator of whether you should receive a test or go to the hospital.

Contact Tracing

This part of the website allows a user to upload the locations that they have visited over the last couple of weeks to assess their exposure to known individuals who had COVID-19 and were in those areas. Based on the locations you have visited and the people who have visited those locations, users will be advised to take the Symptom Analysis test on the COVID contact website and will also be offered information about testing locations around them. This feature is not a subsitute for an actual medical test, but will be a good indicator of whether you should receive a test or go to the hospital.

Location Upload

Location Upload is for users who have or had COVID-19 and would like to inform people of their past whereabouts while they had the virus. They can list all the locations that they have been to while they were known to have the coronavirus, and then choose what locations they have been to with the virus. This will update the database that the Contact Tracing feature references.
